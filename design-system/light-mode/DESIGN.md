# Neo-Brutalist Vibrant System

## Design Vision
This design system utilizes a high-contrast, vibrant palette combined with a sharp, neo-brutalist aesthetic. It prioritizes clarity and a technical, modern feel through geometric typography and uncompromisingly angular shapes.

## Foundation

### Color Palette
- **Primary Color**: #d8b4fe (Lavender) - Used for primary actions and brand presence.
- **Secondary Color**: #98ffd9 (Mint) - Used for supporting UI elements and accents.
- **Tertiary Color**: #ea4c88 (Deep Pink) - Used for highlights, badges, and decorative flair.
- **Neutral Background**: #fdfcf0 (Warm Bone) - A non-white surface that provides a softer foundation for high-chroma elements.

### Typography
The system employs **Space Grotesk** for all typographic roles—headlines, body text, and labels. This monospaced-influenced sans-serif provides a futuristic, technical rhythm throughout the interface.

### Shape and Form
- **Roundedness**: 0 (Sharp). All components—including buttons, cards, and inputs—feature 90-degree corners. This evokes a structural, digital-first aesthetic inspired by early web design and modern brutalism.

### Layout and Space
- **Spacing**: 2 (Normal). The system maintains a balanced density. While the shapes are sharp and the colors are bold, the spacing ensures that elements have enough room to breathe without feeling overly editorial or excessively cramped.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Sharp corners, primary lavender background, high-contrast borders.
- **Surfaces**: Flat, neutral #fdfcf0 backgrounds with vibrant accents.
- **Text**: Space Grotesk with consistent tracking and geometric alignment.
