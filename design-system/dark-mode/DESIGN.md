# Cyber-Geometric Design System

## Core Aesthetic
This design system utilizes a high-contrast, technical aesthetic characterized by sharp edges and a futuristic, monospace-inspired typography. The visual language is built around a "Neo-Brutalist" digital framework, prioritizing clarity and geometric precision over soft affordances.

## Color Palette
The system operates in a deep dark mode, providing a high-contrast foundation for vibrant, electric accents.
- **Primary Color**: #d8b4fe (Electric Lavender) - Used for primary actions and brand presence.
- **Secondary Color**: #98ffd9 (Mint Neo) - Utilized for success states, secondary interactive elements, and UI highlights.
- **Tertiary Color**: #ea4c88 (Dystopian Pink) - Reserved for badges, notifications, and decorative emphasis.
- **Neutral Base**: #121212 (True Dark) - The foundation for all surfaces and background layers.

## Typography
The system exclusively uses **Space Grotesk** across all levels of hierarchy. This typeface provides a technical, fixed-width appearance that enhances the "command center" feel of the interface.
- **Headlines**: Bold and striking, emphasizing the geometric nature of the letterforms.
- **Body & Labels**: Clear and utilitarian, ensuring legibility within complex data environments.

## Layout and Geometry
- **Roundedness**: 0 (Sharp). There are no rounded corners in this system. All buttons, cards, and containers feature strict 90-degree angles to maintain a rigid, architectural structure.
- **Spacing**: 2 (Normal). The layout follows a balanced density, moving away from an editorial sprawl to a more functional, information-rich density that suits productivity and technical applications.

## Component Principles
1. **Hard Edges**: Every UI element must respect the zero-radius constraint.
2. **Chromatic Depth**: Use the secondary and tertiary colors sparingly to guide the eye through the dark environment.
3. **Structured Density**: Maintain a clean, professional grid that balances whitespace with content efficiency.
